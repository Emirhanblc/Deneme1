## Content Types & Distribution

### Cornerstone Content (High-Impact)

- **Frequency:** 1 per quarter
- **Length:** 2000+ words
- **Promotion:** Heavy social media, newsletter feature, internal linking hub

### Regular Articles

- **Frequency:** Bi-weekly
- **Length:** 800-1500 words
- **Promotion:** Standard social media, newsletter inclusion

### Quick Tips & Updates

- **Frequency:** As needed
- **Length:** 300-500 words
- **Promotion:** Social media only

## SEO & Growth Strategy

### Internal Linking

- Each article includes 3-5 high-value internal links
- Cornerstone articles serve as linking hubs
- Related posts sections on all articles

### Social Media Promotion

- LinkedIn: Technical deep-dives, professional audience
- Twitter: Quick tips, code snippets, community engagement
- Reddit: Relevant subreddits (r/linuxadmin, r/devops)

### Newsletter Growth

- ConvertKit integration for email capture
- Weekly newsletter with latest content
- Exclusive tips for subscribers

## Performance Tracking

### Key Metrics

- **Organic Traffic:** Monthly growth target: +15%
- **Newsletter Subscribers:** Target: 1000 by end of Q3
- **Social Shares:** Average 50+ shares per cornerstone article
- **Comment Engagement:** Active discussion on technical topics

### Content Refresh Schedule

- Review and update cornerstone content every 6 months
- Update technical tutorials when software versions change
- Monitor search rankings for target keywords
